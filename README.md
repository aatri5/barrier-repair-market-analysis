# Case Study: Launching a Barrier-Repair Serum for Urban India
**Objective:** To analyze the market viability, consumer demand, and strategic positioning for a new skincare launch in India's "Masstige" segment.

## 🎯 The "Why" (Business Rationale)
The Indian skincare market is evolving from basic hydration to "Derm-ceuticals." Urban Indian women face unique skin stress from PM 2.5 pollution and over-use of harsh actives (Retinol/Acids). 

### The Opportunity
- **Climate Gap:** Most barrier products are heavy creams; India needs lightweight, layerable serums.
- **Price Gap:** Premium results are currently locked behind luxury price tags (₹2000+). 
- **The Mission:** Propose a high-efficacy solution in the ₹500–₹800 range.
